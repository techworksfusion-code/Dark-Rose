The Journey

Dark Rose Tattoos began as a passion project in 2019, born from a deep appreciation for both tattoo artistry and Hindu spiritual iconography. The founder, having studied traditional Indian art forms alongside modern tattooing techniques, saw an opportunity to bridge sacred imagery with contemporary tattoo culture.

Specializing in Lord Shiva designs—from Nataraja to Ardhanarishvara, from Trishula to the sacred Lingam—we approach each piece with reverence. Our blackwork and dotwork techniques honor the gravity of these symbols while delivering stunning visual impact.

Over 500+ custom designs later, we've attracted clients from across India and abroad, each seeking not just a tattoo but a meaningful piece of art that carries personal and spiritual weight.
